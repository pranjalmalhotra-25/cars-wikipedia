Information About Birds

Species Profile Picture Facts
Humming Bird On an average day, a hummingbird will consume double its body weight.
Kingfishers They primarily feed on fish, but also consume insects and other aquatic creatures.
Owls Owls have several fascinating adaptations, including the ability to rotate their heads up to 270 degrees.
Pelicans Pelicans are large, distinctive water birds known for their throat pouches, which they use to scoop up fish.
Swans A key fact about swans is that they are known for mating for life. They form strong pair bonds and are often seen as symbols of love and loyalty.
